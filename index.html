FAQs about Dry Cleaning

Dry cleaning is a process that cleans clothes and textiles using a chemical solvent rather than water. This method is ideal for delicate fabrics that can't withstand the rough and tumble of a washing machine and dryer.

While many types of fabric benefit from dry cleaning, not all can be treated this way. Fabrics like silk, wool, and certain synthetic fibers are ideal for dry cleaning, whereas others may require specific care instructions.

Our experienced professionals pre-treat stains before the dry cleaning process. We use specialized solvents and techniques based on the type of stain and fabric to ensure the best possible outcome without damaging the material.

We are committed to using environmentally friendly solvents and processes. Our modern methods are designed to minimize environmental impact while delivering exceptional cleaning results.
