About Us

Weeklyn Dry Cleaning is your trusted local dry cleaner in Turramurra, NSW, Australia. With decades of experience, we offer a range of services to meet your needs, including alterations, dry cleaning, and laundry. Our team is dedicated to providing top-quality service and ensuring that your garments and soft furnishings receive the care they deserve.

We specialize in handling delicate fabrics, intricate garments, and special items such as wedding dresses, ski wear, and rugs. Our expertise and dedication to excellence have made us a trusted name in the community. At Weeklyn Dry Cleaning, we understand the value of your clothing and home textiles, and we strive to extend their life and maintain their beauty with every service we offer.

Our mission is to provide unparalleled service that surpasses our customers' expectations. We pride ourselves on our attention to detail, state-of-the-art cleaning techniques, and customer-centric approach. Whether you need alterations, deep cleaning, or pressing, you can count on Weeklyn Dry Cleaning for top-notch service.
