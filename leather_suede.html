Expert Care for Leather & Suede

Weeklyn Dry Cleaners provides professional leather and suede cleaning. Our meticulous process ensures your delicate materials are cared for and restored to their original beauty, extending their longevity.

Our Leather and Suede Cleaning Process

  • Inspection to assess the garment's condition and specific needs.
  • Preparation by removing surface dirt and debris.
  • Stain treatment using safe and effective removers.
  • Delicate cleaning to remove dirt and oils.
  • Moisturisation for leather and revitalisation for suede.
  • Drying and finishing to ensure longevity and appearance.

Benefits of Professional Cleaning

  • Increased lifespan of your garments.
  • Expert care and handling for delicate materials.
  • Revitalised appearance with deep cleaning.
