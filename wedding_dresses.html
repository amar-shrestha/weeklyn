Engrave Your Special Day Forever

Weeklyn Dry Cleaners understands the significance of your wedding dress and the importance of preserving its beauty and sentimental value. Our team is dedicated to providing outstanding wedding dress dry cleaning service.

The Importance of Wedding Dress Dry Cleaning

  • Preserving fabric and embellishments to ensure your dress remains impeccable.
  • Removal of stains and spills to restore your dress's pristine appearance.
  • Odour removal to eliminate any lingering odours.

The Bridal Cleaning Process After Your Big Day

  • Professional evaluation to determine the best cleaning method.
  • Spot treatment to target and remove specific stains and spots.
  • Fabric-specific cleaning techniques to preserve your dress's integrity.
  • Hand finishing and detailing for intricate details.
  • Preservation and packaging in a keepsake box.
