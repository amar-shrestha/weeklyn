Steam Pressing at Weeklyn Dry Cleaning

Weeklyn Dry Cleaners offers professional steam pressing to give your garments a crisp and polished look. Our expert team ensures that each garment is handled with care to bring out the best in its appearance.

The Benefits of Steam Pressing

  • Restores shape and structure to your garments.
  • Removes wrinkles and creases effectively.
  • Gentle on delicate fabrics while ensuring a smooth finish.
  • Sanitizes by killing bacteria and eliminating odours.

Our Process

We use professional-grade steam pressing equipment and take great care to adjust the steam pressure and temperature according to each garment's fabric and design. Our experts carefully press each item to ensure an impeccable finish.
